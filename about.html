The Shhh Group creates graphics, experiences, and design solutions for the art world and forward looking organizations throughout Europe, Asia, and the Americas.
Founded and run by a group of multidisciplinary designers with over 15 collective years of experience across print media, experiential design, product design, and architecture.

Maxim A. Diamond

Principal

Trained as an Industrial Designer, Diamond brings a product designer’s perspective to projects. Over a varied carrier he’s worked with distinctive organizations such as Bloomberg, Proenza Schouler, Bottega Veneta, the New York City Housing Authority, MAC Cosmetics, and the International Space Station.
He has also been a design Educator for almost a decade. He currently teaches at Pratt Institute in Brooklyn, New York, lecturing on applied aesthetics and engineering.


Paula Vilaplana

Art Director

Vilaplana creates forward-looking imagery and environmental designs for preeminent organizations and events. She creates work that is both unexpected and familiar, translating complex projects and ideas into design solutions. This approach has led her to work with the Shanghai Biennale, The Victoria & Albert Museum, the Columbia University Graduate School of Architecture, Planning and Preservation, the Princeton School of Architecture, Storefront for Art Architecture and CA2M among others.
